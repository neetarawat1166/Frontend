GOAL IN LIFE

One must have goal in life, in order to make our life meaningful.And to achieve our goal ,we have to work hard and make ourself better everyday.This is the only mantra to make our dream/goal come true.

Life goals are what we want to achieve, and they're much more meaningful than just 'what we need to accomplish to survive'. Unlike daily routines or short-term objectives, they drive our behaviors over the long run.
